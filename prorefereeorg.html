Pro is essentially tasked with the following -

  • They manage the referee and assistant referee program in professional soccer leagues in the United States and Canada.
  • Help current referees but also develop more professional-quality officials at a younger age, and produce officials who will represent the United States and Canada in FIFA competitions.
  • Use proreferees.com as a soccer referees main resource for education about the laws of the game.

Mission

To supply World Class Match Officials for all professional soccer in North America.

  2. By assigning all the professional leagues in North America they have easy ability to accomplish the vision.
  3. With repetition will lead to the vision.

Vission

To become the example for World Class soccer officials by 2022.

    • How they really improve refereeing

    The reason PRO is so successful with helping soccer referees is because the website offers clear concise information about the laws of the game and new interpretations of the laws. The play of the week that PRO does provides great analysis of one MLS play every week and allows every referee to know the correct call and more importantly the reason behind the call.

    PRO is also so effective because they have referee mentors that travel between the MLS and through the lower divisions of soccer like the NASL or USL. By having mentors travel though these different leagues PRO is creating a pipeline that creates great referees that are ready to step up to the big leagues when it is needed. PRO is the best model for referee development and is soon to be the standard for organizations that educate referees in all sports.
